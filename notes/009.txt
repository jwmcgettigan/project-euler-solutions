Pythagorean Triple
 • consists of three positive integers a, b, and c, such that a^2 + b^2 = c^2.
Pythagoean Triplet
 • a set of three natural numbers, a < b < c, for which, a^2 + b^2 = c^2

Observations:
 • if 'a + b + c = n' then 'c = n - a - b'
 • 'a' can at most be 1/3 of 'n'
 • 'b' can at most be 1/2 of 'n'
